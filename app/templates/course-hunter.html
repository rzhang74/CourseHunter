Course Hunter SP 2017

Our Website is dedicated to help you, students in University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, making it easier to get in the classes you'd love to.

What we do:

  • Automatically tracking open spots for the courses of your choice.
  • Sending you email notices about the availability of your chosen courses.
  • Saving your time and energy trying to get into courses by checking manually. (Which is horrible. I've been there.)

What we don't do:

  • Registering courses for you. (This kind of activity is not allowed by the University)
  • Taking others' spots and give them to you. (This is bad and we simply cannot)

Just register with your email address, set up a password, pick your classes and we will be watching for you! It's that simple!
